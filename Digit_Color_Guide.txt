WHAT ARE THE COLOR CODES?
─────────────────────────
In the "Digit Statistics" section of the app, you see a grid of digits 0 through 9.
Each digit has a background colour and a percentage number. These colours tell you
how “hot” or “cold” that digit has been in recent ticks (price changes).

The colours mean:

   🔴 RED      → The digit is appearing MORE OFTEN than usual (recently very active).
   🟡 YELLOW   → The digit is appearing a bit MORE OFTEN (moderate increase).
   🔵 BLUE     → The digit is appearing LESS OFTEN than usual (recently quiet).
   🟢 GREEN    → The digit is appearing a bit LESS OFTEN (moderate decrease).
   ⚪ NEUTRAL  → The digit's frequency is about normal (no strong change).

The percentages show how often that digit has appeared out of the last 100 ticks
(100 price changes). For example, if digit '5' shows 12.0% and is RED, it means
that in the last 100 ticks, the last digit of the price ended with '5' twelve
times – and it has become even more frequent in the very recent ticks.

HOW ARE THE COLOURS CALCULATED? (For your understanding)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
The bot looks at two time windows:
- Long window: last 100 ticks (base rate)
- Short window: last 20 ticks (recent rate)

If the recent rate is at least 1% higher than the long rate → RED.
If it is 0.5% to 1% higher → YELLOW.
If it is at least 1% lower → BLUE.
If it is 0.5% to 1% lower → GREEN.
Otherwise → NEUTRAL.

This tells you which digits are suddenly becoming popular (RED/YELLOW) or
disappearing (BLUE/GREEN) in the very last few ticks.

HOW TO USE THESE COLOURS FOR MANUAL TRADING
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

The bot already has automatic strategies (Over 1-3, Under 6-8, Even, Odd) that
use these colour rules. But you can also trade manually by looking at the grid
and deciding for yourself.

🔹 OVER 1-3 (Bet that the next digit will be 4,5,6,7,8, or 9)
   – Look at digits 0,1,2,3. If they are RED or YELLOW (hot), it means low digits
     have been appearing a lot – but that often signals they are “overdue” to stop.
     The OVER strategy actually looks for low digits to be weak (BLUE/GREEN) and
     high digits to be strong. For manual trading, a simpler rule:
     If digits 0-3 are mostly NEUTRAL or BLUE/GREEN, and digits 4-9 are mostly
     RED/YELLOW, then bet OVER.

🔹 UNDER 6-8 (Bet that the next digit will be 0,1,2,3,4, or 5)
   – Opposite: If digits 6-9 are mostly NEUTRAL or BLUE/GREEN (weak), and
     digits 0-5 are mostly RED/YELLOW (strong), bet UNDER.

🔹 EVEN (Bet that the next digit will be even: 0,2,4,6,8)
   – Look at the even digits. If most of them are RED/YELLOW and the odd digits
     are BLUE/GREEN, bet EVEN.

🔹 ODD (Bet that the next digit will be odd: 1,3,5,7,9)
   – Opposite: odd digits RED/YELLOW, even digits BLUE/GREEN.

🔹 SPECIFIC DIGIT (Advanced – bet on a single digit)
   – If one particular digit is strongly RED (e.g., 12% and red) and all others
     are neutral or blue, you could try betting that exact digit. But this is
     riskier – the bot does not have a built‑in strategy for single digits.

📌 Important: The colours change with every new tick (every new price update).
   So wait a few seconds to see a consistent pattern before placing a manual trade.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
HOW TO PLACE A MANUAL TRADE IN THE APP
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

1. In the main window, go to the "SMART MANUAL TRADE" section (left side).
2. Choose the contract type: Rise/Fall, Higher/Lower, Touch/No Touch, Even/Odd,
   or Over/Under.
3. Enter the stake amount (how much money you want to risk).
4. Enter the duration (in minutes for Rise/Fall etc., in ticks for Even/Odd and
   Over/Under).
5. Click the appropriate button (e.g., BUY, SELL, EVEN, OVER) based on your
   analysis of the colour grid.

Example: If you see digits 0-3 are mostly blue/green and digits 4-9 are mostly
red/yellow, you would choose "Over/Under" contract, then click "OVER 1-3".

The bot will send the trade to Deriv and show the result in the log.

TIPS FOR BEGINNERS
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
- Start with small stakes (e.g., $1) until you understand how the colours behave.
- Watch the digit grid for a minute or two before trading – look for a clear trend.
- Do not rely on colours alone; combine with your own judgement.
- The bot's "Adaptive" mode automatically switches strategies based on these
  colours – you can enable that and let the bot trade for you.
- Manual trading is best for learning; for consistent results, let the bot run
  in Auto-Trade or Adaptive mode.
